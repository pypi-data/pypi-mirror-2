.. class:: Note(*arguments, **keywords)

    Note class for notes (not rests or unpitched elements) that can be represented by one or more notational units A Note knows both its total duration and how to express itself as a set of tied notes of different lengths. For instance, a note of 2.5 quarters in length could be half tied to eighth or dotted quarter tied to quarter.
